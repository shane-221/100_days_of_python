Steps to complete Trading News Alert Program

1) Pull the current stock price for companies( at current)

2) Look at the market price at close

3) Analyse it: Check if the price has gone up or not + Check the percentage rise or fall

4) Pull the news for the Extraordinary rise or fall of items( say 10% rise or fall)

5) Send an email or SMS: What is the Big fluctuation and what was the news relevant to this.
